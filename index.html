Connection & Driver Disclaimer

To successfully program your ESP32 board directly from the browser, your computer needs the correct USB-to-UART bridge drivers installed. Without these drivers, your system will not recognize the COM port of your device.

If your device is not detected when you click "Connect & Flash", please download and install the driver corresponding to your board's USB chip below, then restart your browser.
